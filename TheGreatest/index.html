An official award that this website won

Junha is here to tell you that this website is the greatest, and that if you picked option 2 you clearly know nothing about what a good website looks like. A good website has lots of things that make it great, and I'm here to tell you what those things are. Get ready folks.



1. Uses OBSOLETE html tags


Obsolete, more like, obso-ELITE!!!!11

As you can see, the header for this website uses the obsolete <marquee> tag that makes it go all spinny and fun. You may think this is a bad thing since this website could just die at any moment, but here at Junha HQ we embrace risks and we aren't afraid of bad code. Only using stable tags and libraries is the mark of cowardice, and the way we subvert that here at Junha HQ is part of this websites identity.

Want to spice up your website with your own obsolete tags? Well after a bit of quick research Junha has some for you!

  • <marquee> adds super cool scrolly text! Wow!
  • The <b> and <i> tags are actually outdated and replaced with <strong> and <em> respectively. Use these outdated tags to stick it to the rest of the internet!
  • There are probably more IDK
